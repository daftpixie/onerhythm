# Launch Content Inventory

This document defines the initial publishable content set for OneRhythm launch.

It is intentionally small. The goal is to ship a coherent public narrative and educational surface, not a large backlog of half-reviewed pages.

## Launch set

### Why OneRhythm Exists

- Route: `/stories/why-onerhythm-exists`
- Kind: `essay`
- Status: ready
- Source posture: founder narrative
- Disclaimer required: no

Purpose:

- explain the founder origin
- name the problem clearly
- distinguish the project’s purpose from medical diagnosis

### The Invisible Crisis

- Route: `/campaigns/the-invisible-crisis`
- Kind: `campaign_page`
- Status: ready
- Source posture: founder narrative plus campaign strategy
- Disclaimer required: no

Purpose:

- frame the hidden emotional and mental health burden around arrhythmias
- explain why OneRhythm treats that burden as a public issue

### What the Research Shows

- Route: `/research/what-the-research-shows`
- Kind: `research_translation`
- Status: ready as a launch pattern
- Source posture: reviewed institutional/editorial sources, ready for future external-study expansion
- Disclaimer required: yes

Purpose:

- establish the research translation pattern
- show how OneRhythm explains reviewed material in plain language
- preserve non-diagnostic boundaries visibly

### Community / Heart Mosaic

- Route: `/campaigns/community-heart-mosaic`
- Kind: `campaign_page`
- Status: ready
- Source posture: campaign strategy plus public mosaic/community architecture
- Disclaimer required: no

Purpose:

- explain the Heart Mosaic as collective proof of solidarity
- explain why it is artistic, anonymous, and non-diagnostic

### Questions for Your Doctor

- Route: `/support/questions-for-your-doctor`
- Kind: `support_resource`
- Status: ready
- Source posture: public product and educational-architecture sources
- Disclaimer required: yes

Purpose:

### Mental Health & Support Resources

- Route: `/support/mental-health-support-resources`

## Source and metadata rules

For every launch page:

- keep `publish_date` and `updated_date` populated
- keep `review_state` visible in content metadata
- attach structured `source_links`
- keep founder narrative distinct from research translation
- require the persistent disclaimer on educational surfaces

## Current gaps after this launch set

These should be handled after the initial launch set is stable:

- more condition-specific modules beyond the initial atrial fibrillation page
- a broader external research registry with verified peer-reviewed sources
- locale-specific content records
- richer campaign sequencing beyond the first public narrative set
- dedicated caregiver-specific support pages

## Launch posture

This inventory is realistic by design:

- enough content to explain why the product exists
- enough educational material to be useful
- enough provenance to be trustworthy
- not so much volume that review quality falls apart
