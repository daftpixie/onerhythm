# Content QA Checklist

Use this checklist for every public-facing mission, educational, advocacy, research, and community page before release.

## Scope

Applies to:

- homepage
- about / mission / evidence / learn / community pages
- educational pages
- research translation pages
- support-resource pages
- campaign pages
- public community stories

## Release gate

Mark each item:

- `pass`
- `needs revision`
- `launch blocker`

Any `launch blocker` means the page does not ship.

## Safety and boundary review

- The page does not diagnose, imply diagnosis, or interpret ECGs.
- The page does not recommend treatment, medication changes, or clinical next steps beyond questions someone may bring to a clinician.
- Educational copy is clearly framed as educational, not clinical guidance.
- Founder narrative is not presented as medical evidence.
- Research summary is clearly distinct from personal narrative or advocacy copy.

Launch blocker if:

- the page implies diagnosis, treatment recommendation, or ECG interpretation
- the page blurs founder story and research authority

## Disclaimer review

- Persistent disclaimer is present on every educational surface.
- Disclaimer is visible without relying on dismissal or expansion.
- Disclaimer language is measured and non-alarmist.
- Disclaimer remains readable at mobile sizes and in high contrast.

Launch blocker if:

- an educational page is missing the disclaimer
- the disclaimer is dismissible

## Source and provenance review

- Claims tied to research, guidelines, or institutions link back to structured sources.
- Publish date, update date, and review state are present where expected.
- Source cards or provenance metadata render correctly.
- No claim is stronger than the cited source supports.

Launch blocker if:

- a factual medical-adjacent claim is unsourced
- source links are broken or misleading

## Accessibility review

- Headings follow a sensible hierarchy.
- Links and buttons are keyboard reachable.
- Contrast meets the current design-system rules.
- Images and visual centerpiece sections have text alternatives where needed.
- Motion is restrained and reduced-motion safe.

Launch blocker if:

- key navigation is not keyboard accessible
- contrast failure affects critical content or disclaimers

## Tone and brand review

- Voice is warm, precise, empowering, and never clinical.
- The page names the problem clearly without spectacle.
- Copy avoids startup hype, miracle framing, and vague promises.
- The Heart Mosaic is described as artistic and communal, not diagnostic.

Launch blocker if:

- tone becomes alarmist, salesy, or clinical
- mosaic language implies medical findings

## Privacy and narrative review

- No identifying personal information appears on public pages unless intentionally public and reviewed.
- Public community story pages honor display-name or pseudonym rules.
- Personal narratives do not accidentally expose private third-party details.

Launch blocker if:

- a page exposes private personal data
- a story violates its approved display rule

- Final decision: `ship` / `hold`
