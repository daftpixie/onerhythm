# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and constructive community for everyone who participates in OneRhythm.

This project exists in service of people navigating real health-related fear, uncertainty, and isolation. We expect contributors to communicate with care, empathy, and professionalism.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and considerate
- Assume good intent, but listen when impact is raised
- Give feedback that is clear and constructive
- Welcome contributors of different backgrounds and experience levels
- Keep discussion focused on improving the project

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or intimidation
- Personal attacks or insulting language
- Discriminatory or exclusionary behavior
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Sexualized language or unwelcome advances
- Trolling, derailing, or bad-faith engagement

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:

- repository discussions
- issues and pull requests
- project chat spaces
- public or private communication representing the project

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

They may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately once a project security/community address is established.

Until then, open a private channel through GitHub maintainers if available rather than posting sensitive reports publicly.
